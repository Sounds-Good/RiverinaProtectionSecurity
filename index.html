Our Services

Static Security

Our highly trained security guards provide a visible deterrent and a professional presence at your property. We offer customized static security solutions, including:

  • Gatehouse security
  • Concierge services
  • Access control
  • Loss prevention

Mobile Patrols

Our mobile patrols conduct regular checks of your property to deter criminal activity and ensure your peace of mind. Our services include:

  • Regular checks of buildings and grounds
  • Rapid response to alarms or incidents
  • Detailed surveillance and reporting

Alarm Response

We provide a rapid response to alarm activations, ensuring your property is secure and any incidents are dealt with swiftly. Our alarm response services include:

  • Immediate investigation of the alarm cause
  • Liaison with emergency services if required
  • Securing the property

Crowd Control

We ensure the safety and security of your event with our professional crowd control services. Our experienced team can manage:

  • Ticket checking and access control
  • Effective queue management
  • Conflict resolution
  • Emergency evacuation procedures

Construction & Gatehouse Security

We offer specialized security solutions for construction sites, safeguarding your valuable assets and ensuring a safe working environment. Our services include:

  • Access control for workers and vehicles
  • 24/7 monitoring of equipment and materials
  • Prevention of theft and vandalism
